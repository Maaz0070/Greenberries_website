What Makes greenlight insurance Different?

greenlight insurance, a decentralized service, beleives your money belongs to you. Unlike interest-based conventional insurance, member premiums are invested and the returns are paid back to you via smart contracts! In this model, instead of a third-party providing the insurance, our invite-only members insure each other in times of loss either through the premium pool, investments or deductibles - so you never have to worry about claim denials!

Premiums Act as Investment

Your monthly premiums are pooled together with other members, a portion is invested, and the profits are returned to you! We simply collect a percentage of proifts to cover fees

Accident? Guaranteed Coverage

Unlike conventional insurance, in which the insurance company decides wether a claim is paid out, our members insure each other - either through the reserves, investments, or deductibles.

Fully Transparent

With our blockchain technology and mobile app, you can see our premium pool, investments, and paid out claims so you know nothing shady is going on!

Accidents Don’t Raise Your Premium

Have peace of mind, we never raise premiums after paying out claims.
